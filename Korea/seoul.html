Seoul

Things to Do in Seoul

  • Street Food
    Try delicious street food at Gwangjang Market. From savory tteokbokki to sweet hotteok, the vibrant stalls offer a wide range of flavors that showcase the rich culinary culture of Korea. It's a food lover's paradise!
  • Visit Palaces
    Explore the beautiful palaces like Gyeongbokgung, where you can witness the changing of the guard ceremony and wander through stunning gardens. The architecture and history make it a perfect place for cultural immersion.
