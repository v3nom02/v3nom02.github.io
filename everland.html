Everland Amusement Park

Everland Amusement Park is South Korea's largest theme park, offering a variety of attractions, rides, and entertainment for visitors of all ages. Experience thrilling rides, delicious food, and amazing shows all in one place!

Top Things to Do at Everland:

  • Rides
    Experience thrilling rides like roller coasters and water rides! Everland offers attractions for all ages, from gentle rides for kids to adrenaline-pumping experiences for thrill-seekers. Don't miss the famous T-Express, one of the steepest wooden roller coasters in the world!
  • Food
    Enjoy delicious snacks and meals throughout the park, featuring a wide array of culinary delights. From Korean street food to international cuisine, there's something to satisfy every palate. Be sure to try the signature dishes that make Everland a delightful experience for food lovers!
  • Shows
    Catch amazing live shows featuring various performances! From dazzling parades to theatrical productions, Everland hosts a variety of entertainment options throughout the day. Experience the magic of live performances that create unforgettable memories for visitors of all ages!
