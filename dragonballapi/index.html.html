Welcome to the Dragon Ball Universe

About Dragon Ball

Dragon Ball is an epic tale of adventure, camaraderie, and the endless pursuit of strength. Follow Goku and his friends as they explore the universe, fight incredible battles, and protect the Earth from unimaginable threats.

Meet the Characters

Goku
The Saiyan raised on Earth, striving to be the strongest fighter in the universe.
Vegeta
The proud Prince of Saiyans, determined to surpass Goku at any cost.
Bulma
A genius scientist and Goku's first ally on his journey.
Piccolo
A Namekian warrior who evolves from a villain to a loyal friend.
Trunks
The son of Vegeta and Bulma, a skilled swordsman and fighter.
Frieza
One of the most powerful and feared villains in the universe.
Cell
A bio-engineered android who seeks to achieve perfection and destroy the world.
Majin Buu
A powerful creature who can absorb others and change forms.
Beerus
The God of Destruction, feared by all who cross his path.
Whis
Beerus' attendant and a powerful angel with immense fighting abilities.
Gohan
The eldest son of Goku, known for his immense power and intellect.
Krillin
Goku's best friend and a brave warrior, despite being a human.
Android 18
A powerful android who becomes an ally of Goku and his friends.
Yamcha
A former desert bandit and a member of the Z Fighters.
