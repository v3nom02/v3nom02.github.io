Namsan Tower

Namsan Tower is a must-visit landmark in Seoul, offering stunning views of the city and a range of exciting attractions. Explore the beautiful scenery, experience the unique culture, and create unforgettable memories!

    Leave your love lock on the fence for a memorable experience! Couples from around the world attach locks to symbolize their love and commitment. It's a beautiful sight filled with countless colors and messages, making it a must-see when visiting Namsan Tower.
