Gyeongbokgung Palace

Gyeongbokgung Palace, the largest of the Five Grand Palaces, is a symbol of Korean heritage and architecture.

Top Attractions at Gyeongbokgung Palace:

  • Guard Ceremony
    Experience the changing of the guard ceremony at Gyeongbokgung Palace, which takes place every hour. This colorful event showcases traditional Korean attire and military drills.
  • Geunjeongjeon Hall
    The main throne hall, Geunjeongjeon, is where important state affairs were held. Admire its beautiful architecture and intricate decorations.
  • National Folk Museum
    Located within the palace grounds, this museum offers insights into the everyday life of Koreans throughout history. Discover various exhibits and artifacts.
